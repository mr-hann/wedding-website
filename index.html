Our Story

It truly started in a way we didn’t expect.
Marvell: On November 13th, 2023, our paths crossed in a season where neither of us was thinking about marriage. It was the last thing on our minds. But somewhere between conversations and quiet moments, something meaningful began to grow. We found ourselves drawn into long conversations about God, purpose, destiny, and ministry. What started as simple interactions soon became intentional prayer walks, hand in hand, singing praises and songs of faith, building something deeper than we even realized at the time. We just enjoyed each others company so much and ensured we never missed the moment.

Yomi speaking, my defining moment was when i shared my heart about recent struggles in business and my passion for souls (especially Young Adults and Teenagers alike) Marvell's response came through Scripture: Ephesians 3:20 (TPT), I had read this Scripture time and time again, but that night over the phone when she explained it, it felt like i had never read or heard of this verse before. In that moment, something settled in my heart. I knew she was going to be in my life a long time. I knew this was a friendship that would last.
Marvell it was his mystery, consistency, and integrity that drew me close and kept me. These are qualities that felt rare and deeply reassuring, especially for my kind of person.
Marvell: We were good friends… but soon, we sensed that God desired something more for us, and He had asked that we do not pervert what He was brewing on our insides. Lol.

Yomi: So we went back to Him in prayer and fasting, seeking clarity and alignment. On January 29th, 2024, we received that clarity. God approved of us. With that conviction, we began our courtship officially on March 29th, and the rest has been a journey of intentional love and purpose.
Marvell: For us, it has never just been about what we want, but about what God wants. Every step has been guided by one question: Does this please God? We’ve learned that marriage is more than the love and butterflies. For us, It is purpose, partnership. It is two chasing ten thousand. We chose each other because our dreams, our vision, and our values align and more importantly, because they align with God’s will for us.

Yomi: We love and honour each other deeply and wholeheartedly. But we are constantly reminded that this is not just about us it is about what God wants to do through us and in us.
Marvell Our love is still unfolding. It is sacrifice. It is dying to self daily. It is clarity. It is a story God is writing one day at a time. And with each new day. we choose to begin again
